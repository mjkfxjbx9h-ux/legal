Weldwise Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Weldwise (the “app”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app processes.

Data we collect

None. Weldwise does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device — no account is required, and no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

Purchases

The optional one-time “Weldwise Pro” unlock is processed by the Apple App Store. Weldwise never sees your payment information; the transaction is handled entirely by Apple.

Deleting your data

Because everything stays on your device, deleting the app permanently removes any data it stored.

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
