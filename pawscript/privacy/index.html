Pawscript Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Pawscript ("the app") is built privacy-first. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing. Pawscript does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Every record you create — your pets, their medications, vaccines, symptoms, weight and notes — is stored only on your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Purchases

Pawscript Pro is offered as an auto-renewing subscription and as a one-time "Lifetime" purchase. All purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. Pawscript never sees or stores your payment information; the entire transaction is handled by Apple.

Your data, exported by you

Pro features let you generate a PDF report or export your data so you can share it with your vet. These files are created on your device and are shared only when you choose to share them. We never receive a copy.

Deleting your data

Because all data lives on your device, deleting the app permanently and completely removes all of your data.

Children's privacy

Pawscript is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
